**Privacy**

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

It does not use analytics, tracking technologies, cookies, or third-party services.

All data handled by the app (such as saved devices or settings) is stored **locally on your device only**, using standard Android storage mechanisms.

These data never leave your phone and can be removed at any time by uninstalling the app.

The app does not connect to any external servers other than the devices you explicitly configure on your local network.
