Control computers on your local network by sending **Wake-on-LAN** and **remote shutdown** commands
from your Android device.

This app is designed to be simple, fast, and fully local: No accounts, no cloud services, no external servers.

It works entirely within your local network and gives you direct control over your machines with minimal configuration.
